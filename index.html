Frequently Asked Questions

What's the mission of Confident?

Here at Confident, we believe that an active lifestyle with a balanced diet and a positive attitude is the key to a happier life. That's why we designed Confident, an all in one solution with everything you will need to build your stress-free and confident you.

What makes Confident different?

There are thousands of health & fitness apps in the store. The difference between us and them is that we aren't just an app, but rather a movement. A movement whose goal is to build our self-confidence with our mind and body and won't stop until reaching that goal.

Who's behind Confident?

A team of the most successful health & fitness apps has gathered and spin-off to create the ultimate movement embodied in an app - The Confident app. You will learn more about us as we release Confident.

Who are the mentors & athletes?

Our mentors and athletes are handpicked and the best in the industry. They have all physical, nutritional, and mental expertise needed to pack Confident with rich and engaging content, so you can follow along and reach your success.

When is Confident coming out?

Dozens of teams are working around the clock to build Confident and make it available to the public. We are finalizing the release so join the waiting list to be the first to know when we go live.

How can I become more involved in Confident?

As we said, Confident isn't just an app, but a living and breathing movement. You can become a Confident ambassador by contacting us at ambassadors@weconfident.com and our team will provide you a starter pack to spread Confident awesomeness.
